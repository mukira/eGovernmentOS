## üíº For Cabinet Secretaries: Policy Impact

**The Problem**: Policies are made in a vacuum. A CS doesn't know how a decision in their Ministry affects another Ministry until it's too late.

**The E-Nation OS Solution**:
*   **Cross-Ministry Correlation**: The CS for Agriculture can see how a fertilizer subsidy impacts the CS for Trade's food price index.
*   **Performance Tracking**: Real-time monitoring of all projects and parastatals under their portfolio. No more "waiting for the monthly report."
*   **Automated Briefings**: The AI Engine generates a daily "Morning Brief" summarizing every critical development, news story, and risk factor relevant to their docket.

## üìù For Principal Secretaries: Operational Efficiency

**The Problem**: PSs are drowning in paperwork. They sign thousands of documents without the time to read or analyze them deeply.

**The E-Nation OS Solution**:
*   **AI Document Review**: The AI scans contracts, tenders, and memos for risks, anomalies, and compliance issues instantly.
*   **Fraud Detection**: Before authorizing a payment, the system checks IFMIS data, supplier history, and E-Citizen records to flag potential ghost suppliers.
*   **Resource Optimization**: Data-driven allocation of budget and personnel based on real-time needs, not historical guesses.
